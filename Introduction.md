# The Best Neighborhood in Pittsburgh Project
## Introduction
The Best Neighborhood in Pittsburgh project is designed to as its name suggests discover the best neighborhood in Pittsburgh PA, to live in. In case you are unaware Pittsburgh is a city, located in Allegheny county and is home to 90 distinct and unique neighborhoods all with something new to offer. We have created three metrics in order to judge and compare the neighborhoods. We have based these metrics off of data provided by the https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/ website.
